The Kumbh Mela is held in each city in 3 year rotation, repeating every 12 years. As you can see there
is a "small" Magh Mela every year in Allahabad during the month of Magh (mid January - mid February). The
Ardh (half) Kumbh Mela is held at both Allahabad and Hardwar. The Maha Kumbh Mela is only in Allahabad,
once in 12 years (next in 2013).
